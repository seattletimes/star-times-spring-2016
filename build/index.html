Three times a year, The Seattle Times honors the best athletes and coaches in Seattle-area high-school team sports with its Star Times all-area teams. The winter selections are for boys' and girls' basketball.

Seattle Times staffers and freelancers select Star Times all-area honorees from athletes in King and Snohomish counties, plus schools in the Metro and WesCo conferences. Meet the winter 2016 selections below.
